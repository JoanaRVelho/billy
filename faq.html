What is a SAF-T file?

Standard Audit File for Tax (SAF-T) is an international normalized file in XML format, for exchanging reliable accounting data from organizations to the national tax authority or external auditors. The Standard is defined by the Organization for Economic Cooperation and Development (OCED) and it enables auditors to access accounting data in an easy readable format. SAF-T incorporates exported accounting data from an original accounting system, for a given time period and by virtue of its uniform layout and format it's easily readable and extensible according to need. It has been widely adopted in Europe, being extended as needed in respect of the policy, legislation and administration of each country.
