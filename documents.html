Documentation

You can access the Javadoc of Billy current release in the links above:

  • Billy-Core
  • Billy-Core-Jpa
  • Billy-Gin
  • Billy-Platypus

You also can made your javadoc of your Billy's version by executing the following command in the main folder: 

$ mvn javadoc:javadoc
Then the documentation javadoc will be generated in /billy/billy-< project >/target/site
